    Human capital

    Lack of Demand
    Lack of demand (or unknown or uncertain demand) for a certain type of governance data or data for a particular country.
    Interopeability
    The data was of such a different type and format that it was difficult to use it effectively alongside other data or information.
    Mandate

    Regularity of coverage
    Was this data at least for some of the countries available for limited number of years in the past? Or may be most recent data was not available? Insufficient and sporadically produced data? Certain countries have never been covered for this governance data?

    Technical capacity
